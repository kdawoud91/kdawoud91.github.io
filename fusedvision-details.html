CVPR Workshops, 2025 • Khaled Dawoud et al.

Anomaly DetectionComputer VisionKnowledge FusionReal‑world

Overview

FusedVision addresses the gap between lab‑bench anomaly detection and messy, real‑world deployments. It infuses domain knowledge and multi‑signal cues into vision models to improve robustness, reduce false alarms, and support practical inspection scenarios.

Focus
  • Knowledge‑infused detection
  • Robustness & precision
  • Industrial applicability
Methods
  • Feature fusion (vision + priors)
  • Lightweight adapters
  • Thresholding with context
